about michael

With so many talented individuals out there, why hire me?

My brutally honest take: I am a self-learner, through and through. I don’t know everything, but I have the powerful and valuable skill of being able to properly Google for the right answers. I understand that it isn’t possible to memorize all of the ins and outs of a programming language. But I am very passionate about writing efficient and functional code. I work hard on my projects and continue to develop my skills outside of work using Udemy. If there is a skill I must develop as I work, I look on Udemy.

If you have made it this far, you may have asked yourself, "How can this guy help my company?. It is important to understand that I probably fit most of your requirements wish list or you wouldn’t be wasting your time here. I know I don’t need to have every answer on the tip of my tongue. However, I will always seek the answer through peer-to-peer interactions or using Google-Fu. I will always deliver a working, efficient product. The delivery is the most important part, because that is likely what will be seen by the people that you, the leader/manager, must answer to.
